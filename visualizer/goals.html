General Trauma Management

Definition

An injury caused by a physical force. details 3
  • D1 A bodily injury that may encompass a large range of severity
  • D2 Often the consequence of motor vehicle crashes, falls, drowning, gunshots, fires and burns, stabbings, or blunt assaults
  • D3 Severe enough to cause death or disability

Patient Care Goals

Rapid, safe, and appropriate injury management and transport. details 3
  • G1 Rapid assessment and management of life-threatening injuries
  • G2 Safe movement of patient to prevent worsening injury severity
  • G3 Rapid and safe transport to the appropriate level of trauma care

Patient Presentation

Inclusion

  • Low blood glucose + symptoms, OR
  • Altered level of consciousness, OR
  • Stroke symptoms, OR
  • Seizure, OR
  • History of diabetes + other medical symptoms, OR
  • Pediatric patient with suspected alcohol ingestion

Exclusion

  • Cardiac Arrest
